INDIAN ARCHITECTURE

Destination Name: DELHI

Location: INDIA

Brief Description:

Mughal architecture is all about those intricate designs,
like those mesmerizing geometric patterns and those onion-like domes on their buildings.
And they loved using marble and red sandstone for that elegant look.

Taj Mahal in Agra: For his cherished wife Mumtaz, the emperor Shah Jahan constructed a magnificent white marble mausoleum.
Visitors come here to enjoy its breathtaking beauty and to experience the romance that inspired it.
Amazing carvings and color-changing effects in response to sunshine.

Red Fort in Delhi: The Red Fort, as its name suggests, is a sizable fort in Delhi.
It was once the Mughal rulers' primary residence.
The architecture is remarkable, with its tall walls and lovely gates.
It is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a fantastic location to learn about history.
