Top Attractions

TAJ MAHAL

People from all over the world come to see the Taj Mahal because it's not just a stunning building, it's also a symbol of love.
It's got a romantic story behind it that makes it even more special.
So, if you ever get the chance to go to India, definitely check out the Taj Mahal

RED FORT

In the center of Old Delhi, India, the Red Fort resembles a historical time capsule.
It is a sizable red sandstone fort that rises tall and exudes stories of India's illustrious past.
You can't help but be astounded by its splendor and the amount of history contained inside its walls. .

QUTUB MINAR

the Qutub Minar is basically this tall, ancient tower standing tall in Delhi, India.
It's like a big stone rocket pointing towards the sky.
People often say it's the tallest brick minaret globally, but hey, that's not just a boring record; it's pretty cool!
